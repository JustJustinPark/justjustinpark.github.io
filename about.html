Hi there, I'm Justin!

I am a Korean-American educator, musician, and community organizer currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I made the pivot into teaching after college, studying education at Stanford University and teaching math in San Francisco public high schools for a few years. I now work as a freelance tutor for math, physics, computer science, and music theory! You can learn more about my tutoring services here.

I also give history and art tours of San Francisco Chinatown through the Chinese Culture Center of San Francisco.

Check us out if you're interested!

I am an avid a cappella singer, arranger, and enjoyer! I currently sing with Firecrackers Acapella, and in the past I was in MIT Syncopasian and Stanford O-Tone. I also release my own personal music projects over at Just Music With Justin and also publish my original arrangements online.

In my free time, I like to play board games, card games, capoeira (an Afro-Brazilian martial art), and violin.

Thanks for stopping by ^.^
